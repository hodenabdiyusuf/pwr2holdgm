Things to think about:

Stanford's Open Policing Project

I used some of the data gathered from project to model the probability of what is likely to happen to someone if they are pulled over. I made sure to take into account factors like race and genre of music

The Tyranny of a Traffic Ticket and

Driving While Black

These articles address a lot of hidden factors of racialized policing and subconscious biases. They describe the ways that something that is as menile as a traffic ticket and cause a lot of damage and harm on the recipient just because of the color of their skin or their socioeconomic class. If you would like to read more about this topic, this article provides some insight on research done about "getting off easy" with speeding tickets and the racial biases that come into play there.

How Racism & Bias Against Hip-Hop Cause Lyrics To Be Introduced In Court Cases
